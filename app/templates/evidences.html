Evidences using Performance Metrics

The developed lncRNA disease association prediction model demonstrates exceptional performance, boasting an accuracy of 96.56%. Its precision at 97.12% ensures minimal false positives, while a recall rate of 95.98% captures a significant portion of true positives. With an F1-score of 96.54%, the model maintains a balanced accuracy and precision trade-off. The Matthews Correlation Coefficient (MCC) stands strong at 93.13%, emphasizing its robustness in classification. Notably, the Area Under the Precision-Recall Curve (AUPR) impressively reaches 99.13%, highlighting the model's ability to yield high-confidence predictions. These metrics collectively underscore the reliability and efficacy of this lncRNA disease association prediction model.
